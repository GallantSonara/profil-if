Informatics Major

Welcome to the Department of Informatics at International Women University! We provide a study program aimed at preparing students to become experts in the field of information technology, capable of creating innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Our study program offers a wide range of courses covering software development, computer networking, information security, data processing, and cloud technology. In addition, we also provide opportunities for industry practice and internships, which can help students gain valuable work experience in the real world.

We have highly qualified faculty and teaching staff who have experience and knowledge in the field of information technology. Additionally, we also have modern and up-to-date computer laboratories, which can assist students in conducting research and information technology projects.

We are committed to providing high-quality education and supporting students to achieve their potential in the world of information technology. Join us in the Department of Informatics at International Women University and start your journey towards becoming a successful information technology expert!
